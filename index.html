Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

TAMI (Task & Action Manager) is a personal task management app developed by TAMI Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you sign in and use TAMI, we collect the following information:

  • Google Account information — your name and email address, provided via Google Sign-In
  • Task data — tasks you create, including titles, due dates, categories, recurrence settings, and completion status
  • Device information — basic device data needed to deliver notifications

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and sync your tasks across sessions
  • Send you task reminders and notifications
  • Authenticate your identity securely via Google Sign-In
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service provided by Google. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

Third-Party Services

TAMI uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and data storage (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — secure account authentication (Google Privacy Policy)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to operate the services listed above or as required by law.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. You can delete your tasks at any time within the app. To request complete account deletion, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

TAMI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by signing out and deleting your account

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
